Join A Homam

Join A Homam

Agni metaphorically and physically is a transformative agent causing sublime changes in both the individual and the environment. The significance of Fire rituals/ Homams in overcoming problems both material and spiritual finds mention several times in vedas, the itihasas and the puranas and is aptly quoted by several scholars.

A properly done Fire ritual at the right place by the right person with the right sankalpa is sure to yield results. The common material issues faced by Human kind include things relating to Health, Marriage, career, money, Children, enemies etc and it is perfectly fine to have a dharmic desire to aspire for the things mentioned.

NITYAGNI conducts Homas at the sacred Arunachala kshetra both for material and spiritual upliftment through a team of trained ritwiks.

Annadana Seva

The word Annadanam literally means offering food to the people. People cannot live without food, hence the donors of Annadanam are also considered as donors of Pranadanam. People who will donate for Annadanam would get great honour in the court of the LORD.

One must serve mankind selflessly to get rewards that are beyond your accepted wisdom. When you request for something in front of lord, it gets fulfilled on the basis of your deeds. The deed of Annadanam helps people to truncate their sins.

Sacred Steps

As part of Sacred Steps, Agnimitras circumnavigate (girivalam) Arunachala sacred hill. This program includes satsangs, homa and annadana . Our one and only power-spot is Tiruvannamalai.

Girivalam is a popular religious practice in Tamil Nadu in which devotees circle around the Annamalai Hill on the occasion of full moon every month.
